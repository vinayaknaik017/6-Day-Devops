Health Benefits of Fresh Foods

Why Choose Fresh Vegetables, Dals, Natural Oils, and Natural Ghee?

Consuming fresh and natural foods improves overall health by providing essential nutrients and promoting better digestion, immunity, and vitality. Here are some benefits:

  • Fresh Vegetables: Packed with vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants that boost immunity and reduce the risk of chronic diseases.
  • Dals: Rich in protein, iron, and fiber, dals support muscle growth, heart health, and digestion.
  • Natural Oils: Contain healthy fats that support brain and heart health, along with promoting glowing skin.
  • Natural Ghee: An excellent source of energy, ghee improves digestion, lubricates joints, and strengthens immunity.

List of Vegetables, Vitamins, and Their Benefits

Carrots

Vitamin: A

Benefits: Improves vision, supports immunity, and promotes healthy skin.

Spinach

Vitamin: K, A, C

Benefits: Strengthens bones, boosts immunity, and supports heart health.

Broccoli

Vitamin: C, K

Benefits: Protects against chronic diseases, improves digestion, and supports bone health.

Tomatoes

Vitamin: C, A

Benefits: Reduces the risk of heart diseases and improves skin health.

Bell Peppers

Vitamin: C, B6

Benefits: Enhances immunity, supports brain function, and reduces inflammation.
